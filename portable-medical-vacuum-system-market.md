<p><strong>The Portable Medical Vacuum System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Medical Vacuum System market consists of three types: Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. Dry Claw Pump Technology utilizes a claw-shaped rotor to create a vacuum, ensuring a dry and contaminant-free environment. Dry Rotary Vane Technology uses vanes to compress air and create a vacuum, without the need for oil lubrication. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology utilizes oil lubrication to create a vacuum through the rotation of vanes. These technologies are commonly used in portable medical vacuum systems to provide efficient and reliable vacuum suction.</p></p>
