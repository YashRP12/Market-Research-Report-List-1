<p><p>Micro syringes are widely used in laboratories and research facilities for precise liquid handling. There are two main types of micro syringes: autosampler and manual syringes. Autosampler syringes are typically used in automated systems and are designed for high-throughput sample analysis. They are commonly used in applications such as chromatography. On the other hand, manual syringes are manually operated and are suitable for smaller-scale operations and more specialized applications, such as microinjections. Both types of micro syringes offer accurate and controlled liquid dispensing, catering to different laboratory needs.</p></p>
