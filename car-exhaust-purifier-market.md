<p><p>The car exhaust purifier market consists of two main types: catalytic converters and smoke exhaust filters. Catalytic converters are devices that convert harmful gases, such as carbon monoxide and nitrogen oxide, into less harmful substances through a chemical reaction. They are commonly used in gasoline-powered vehicles. Smoke exhaust filters, on the other hand, are designed to trap and filter particulate matter and harmful smoke emissions from diesel-powered vehicles. Both types of purifiers are essential in mitigating air pollution caused by vehicle emissions.</p></p>
