<p><p>The Vehicle Emission Testers market is divided into various types. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Testers use infrared light to measure gas concentrations. Flame Ionization Detectors detect the presence of hydrocarbon vapor by ionizing the molecules. Smoke Meters measure the quantity of particulate matter emitted by a vehicle. Cross Duct Testers measure emissions by drawing a sample from the exhaust stack. Emission and Combustion Testers with Chillers analyze both the emissions and combustion efficiency of a vehicle. Lastly, there are other types of testers available in the market as well.</p></p>
