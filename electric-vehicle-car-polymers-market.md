<p><p>The electric vehicle polymers market comprises two main types: engineering plastics and elastomers. They offer high strength, heat resistance, and chemical stability. Elastomers are flexible polymers used for applications such as seals, gaskets, and tires, providing excellent elasticity and impact resistance.
