On the other hand, secondary nutrients are necessary in larger quantities, and they include elements like calcium, magnesium, and sulfur. Both types of nutrients are vital for optimizing plant growth and ensuring healthy crops. The fertilizer filler market offers various products that supplement these nutrients, enabling farmers to enhance the nutrient content of their soils and improve crop yields.</p></p>
