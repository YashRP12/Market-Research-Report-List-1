<p><strong>The Panel-mount Temperature Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Controller</li><li>Proportional Controller</li><li>Pid Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The panel-mount temperature controller market consists of three types: switch controller, proportional controller, and PID controller. </p><p>1. Switch controller: This type maintains the temperature by switching the heating or cooling system on or off at a specific set point temperature. It is a simple and cost-effective solution, suitable for applications where minor temperature variations are permissible.</p><p>2. Proportional controller: It adjusts the heating or cooling system's output based on the temperature difference from the desired set point. It provides a more precise control and reduces temperature fluctuations, commonly used in applications that require more accuracy.</p><p>3. PID controller: It stands for proportional, integral, and derivative. This advanced controller constantly measures the temperature and calculates the appropriate output to reach the set point quickly and accurately. It offers the highest level of control precision and is widely used in industrial processes where temperature regulation is critical.</p></p>
