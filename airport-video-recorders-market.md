NVRs are used to record and store video data from network cameras, providing advanced features such as remote access and real-time monitoring. IPVRs are similar but are specifically designed for internet protocol (IP) cameras. DVRs are used for analog cameras and record video onto a hard drive or other storage medium. The "others" category may include specialized recorders or hybrid systems that combine features from different types.</p></p>
