<p><p>The audio chips and modules market can be categorized into different types including audio amplifiers, audio CODECs, audio digital signal processors (DSPs), and audio system-on-chips (SoCs). Audio amplifiers are responsible for amplifying sound signals, while audio CODECs convert analog audio signals to digital and vice versa. Audio DSPs focus on processing and enhancing audio signals, and audio SoCs integrate multiple audio functionalities into a single chip. These different types cater to various needs in the audio industry, providing solutions for better sound quality, signal processing, and integration.</p></p>
