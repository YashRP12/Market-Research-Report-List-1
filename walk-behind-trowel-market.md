<p><p>The walk-behind trowel market can be classified into two types: hydrostatic and mechanical. Hydrostatic trowels are powered by a hydraulic system, utilizing fluid pressure to deliver consistent and smooth operation. They are known for their excellent maneuverability and control. On the other hand, mechanical trowels are powered by a gasoline engine, making them more suitable for larger and more demanding jobs. While mechanical trowels may lack the precision of hydrostatic ones, they are typically more affordable and widely available in the market. Overall, both types cater to different needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
