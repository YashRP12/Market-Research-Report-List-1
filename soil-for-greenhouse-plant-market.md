Garden soil is a natural soil mixture with added compost and topsoil, suitable for garden beds. Peat moss, derived from decomposed plant materials, improves water retention and provides acidity to the soil. Professional soil is a specialized blend designed for commercial use, offering optimum nutrient balance.
