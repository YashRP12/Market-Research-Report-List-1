Non-metallic RTP refers to pipes made from composite materials such as fiber-reinforced thermoplastic and are known for their corrosion resistance, lightweight nature, and flexibility. They find applications in areas like oil and gas, marine, and water treatment industries. On the other hand, Metallic RTP is made from steel or aluminum and is preferred for applications needing high strength, pressure resistance, and extreme temperatures such as offshore oil and gas drilling and deep-sea environments.</p></p>
