<p><p>The concrete transport and distribution system market is divided into three types: mechanical, hydraulic, and manual. Mechanical systems use machines such as conveyors and pumps to transport and distribute concrete, offering high efficiency and speed. Hydraulic systems rely on hydraulic pumps and valves to transport and distribute concrete, providing higher control and accuracy. Manual systems involve labor-intensive processes, where workers transport and distribute concrete using manual tools, offering lower efficiency and productivity than mechanical or hydraulic systems. Overall, these different market types cater to varying needs and preferences in the concrete transport and distribution industry.</p></p>
