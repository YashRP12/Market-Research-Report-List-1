<p><p>LDPE decking is a type of decking material made from low-density polyethylene. It is available in two market types: capped composite and uncapped composite. Capped composite LDPE decking has a protective layer that covers the surface, providing added durability and resistance to stains, fading, and mold. Uncapped composite LDPE decking lacks this protective layer and may be more vulnerable to wear and tear. Both types offer a low-maintenance option for outdoor decking, but capped composite LDPE decking tends to be more expensive due to its enhanced features.</p></p>
