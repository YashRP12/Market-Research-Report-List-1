<p><p>The automotive anti-vibration isolator mounts market is categorized into three types: cylindrical mounts, bushing mounts, and conical mounts. Cylindrical mounts are designed to absorb vibrations in a wide range of frequencies and provide effective damping. Bushing mounts offer superior isolation by minimizing vibration transfer between two components. Conical mounts are designed to provide optimal vibration isolation and can handle heavy loads. All three types of mounts are used in automotive applications to reduce noise, vibration, and harshness, enhancing ride comfort and vehicle performance.</p></p>
