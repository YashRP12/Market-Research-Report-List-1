<p><p>Exhaust analyzers are devices used to measure the emissions from vehicles and industrial processes. There are different types of exhaust analyzers available in the market. The non-dispersive infrared analyzer utilizes infrared radiation to detect and measure the concentration of specific gases. The flame ionization detector operates by ionizing organic compounds in the exhaust gas and measuring the resulting current. Chemiluminescence analyzers utilize the chemical reaction of gases to produce light, which is then measured to determine the gas concentration. Other types of exhaust analyzers include electrochemical analyzers and photoacoustic spectrometry analyzers.</p></p>
