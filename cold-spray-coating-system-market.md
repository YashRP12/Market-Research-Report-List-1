<p><p>Cold spray coating system is a technique used for depositing protective coatings on various materials. The two main types of this system are High Pressure Cold Spraying (HPCS) and Low-Pressure Cold Spraying (LPCS). HPCS operates at higher gas pressures and velocities, resulting in higher coating quality and density. On the other hand, LPCS operates at lower pressure and is more cost-effective but may have lower coating quality. Both systems offer unique advantages depending on the specific coating requirements and budgetary considerations.</p></p>
