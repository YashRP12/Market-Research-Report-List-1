<p><p>Non-thermal processing involves the use of alternative methods to traditional heat treatment for preserving and processing food. High Pressure Processing (HPP) subjects food to intense pressure, while Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) utilizes electricity to inactivate microorganisms. Irradiation involves the use of ionizing radiation, such as gamma rays, to kill harmful bacteria. Ultrasound technology uses sound waves to kill microorganisms and improve food quality. Cold Plasma treatment, on the other hand, uses ionized gas to eliminate pathogens. These non-thermal processing methods offer different benefits in terms of food safety and preservation.</p></p>
