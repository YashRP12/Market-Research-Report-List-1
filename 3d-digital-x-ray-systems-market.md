<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global 3D Digital X-Ray Systems market?</strong></p>
<p><p>One emerging trend in the global 3D digital X-ray systems market is the increasing adoption of cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) technology. CBCT offers a three-dimensional view of the patient's anatomy, providing improved accuracy and detailed imaging, especially for dental and orthopedic applications. Another trend is the integration of advanced artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms into 3D digital X-ray systems, allowing for automated analysis and interpretation of images, reducing the workload for radiologists. Additionally, the market is witnessing a rise in the development of portable and handheld 3D digital X-ray systems, catering to the need for point-of-care imaging in remote locations or during emergencies.</p></p>
