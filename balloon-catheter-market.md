<p><p>The balloon catheter market consists of various types of specialized catheters used in medical procedures. Normal balloon catheters are used for dilation of blocked blood vessels. Cutting balloon catheters have small blades on the balloon surface to help open harder-to-treat blockages. Scoring balloon catheters have tiny grooves on the balloon, providing better expansion. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release medication to prevent reblockage. Stent graft balloon catheters help secure stents in place to treat aneurysms. These different types address specific medical needs, making the balloon catheter market versatile and diverse.</p></p>
