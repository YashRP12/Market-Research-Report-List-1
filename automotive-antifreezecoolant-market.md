<p><p>Automotive antifreeze/coolant is a chemical additive used in the cooling system of vehicles to regulate the temperature of the engine and prevent overheating. The increasing demand for automobiles globally is the major driver for this market. As automotive production and sales continue to rise, the need to maintain engine efficiency and prevent overheating becomes crucial, further propelling the demand for antifreeze/coolant products.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a shift towards the adoption of organic acid technology (OAT) and hybrid organic acid technology (HOAT) coolants. These coolants provide extended service intervals, better engine protection, and compatibility with different types of engines and materials. As more automobile manufacturers recommend or require the use of OAT and HOAT coolants in their vehicles, the market for these products is growing.</p><p>Additionally, the focus on environmental sustainability and regulations related to coolant disposal and emissions has encouraged the development of eco-friendly and biodegradable antifreeze/coolant alternatives. This trend is expected to further drive the market's growth in the coming years.</p><p>The automotive antifreeze/coolant market is estimated to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.3% during the forecast period.
