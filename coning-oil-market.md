<p><p>Coning oil, also known as cone oil or drawing lubricant, is a type of lubricant that is primarily used in the metalworking industry during the drawing or forming process of metal products. Coning oil plays a crucial role in enhancing the productivity and quality of these processes, thereby fueling its demand.</p><p>Additionally, the rising adoption of advanced lubricants in the metalworking industry is further contributing to the growth of the coning oil market. These advancements are anticipated to drive market growth in the future.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing focus on sustainable manufacturing practices and the increasing regulatory pressure to reduce the usage of harmful chemicals are expected to boost the demand for eco-friendly coning oil products.
