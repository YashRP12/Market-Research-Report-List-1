<p><p>Automotive suspension refers to the system of components that connect a vehicle's body to its wheels, providing support, stability, and allowing for relative motion between them. It also helps to maintain tire contact with the road, improving traction and handling.</p><p>The current outlook of the automotive suspension market is positive, with steady growth projected in the coming years. One of the key trends is the growing adoption of advanced suspension systems, such as adaptive or active suspensions, which adjust damping and stiffness based on road conditions and driving dynamics. These advanced systems offer improved comfort, handling, and safety.</p><p>Another significant trend is the rising popularity of electric vehicles (EVs) and autonomous vehicles (AVs).
