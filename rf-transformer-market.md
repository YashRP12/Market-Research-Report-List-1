<p><p>RF transformers are crucial components in radio frequency systems that aid in transmitting, receiving, and transforming signals. Two main types of RF transformers include Magnetic Coupling Transformers and Transmission Line Transformers. Magnetic Coupling Transformers transfer power through magnetic fields between primary and secondary windings, whereas Transmission Line Transformers change voltages and impedances using transmission lines. Both types play significant roles in amplification, impedance matching, and signal attenuation in RF systems, facilitating efficient and reliable communication.</p></p>
