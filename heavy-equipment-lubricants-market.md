General industrial oil is versatile and can be used in various applications such as bearings, compressors, and pumps. Gear oil is designed for gears and provides protection against the high pressures and loads they experience. Transmission and hydraulic fluids are used in transmissions and hydraulic systems to provide smooth and efficient operation.
