The thermoplastic resin type refers to CPE that is used in applications where high flexibility and exceptional resistance to chemicals and weathering are required. On the other hand, the elastomer rubber type is used for applications that demand superior impact resistance, weatherability, and thermal insulation properties. Both types of CPE offer unique characteristics, making them suitable for different applications in industries like automotive, construction, and electrical.</p></p>
