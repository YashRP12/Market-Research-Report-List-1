<p><p>Keypad Electronic Access Control Systems are divided into two market types: Networked Systems and Standalone Systems. Networked Systems allow multiple keypads to be connected to a central control panel, enabling remote access management and real-time monitoring. This type is ideal for large-scale applications and offers advanced functionalities such as integration with other security systems. On the other hand, Standalone Systems operate independently without requiring a central control panel. They are simpler and more cost-effective solutions suitable for smaller installations where remote access management is not necessary.</p></p>
