<p><p>The electric utility vehicle market offers three main types: Acid Lead Type, Gel Lead Type, and Lithium Ion Type. Acid Lead Type vehicles use acid-filled lead-acid batteries, which are cost-effective but have limited lifespan and require regular maintenance. Gel Lead Type vehicles use gel-filled lead-acid batteries, offering improved lifespan and requiring less maintenance. Lithium Ion Type vehicles utilize lithium-ion batteries, which are lightweight, have longer lifespan, and require minimal maintenance. Despite being more expensive, lithium-ion technology is growing in popularity due to its superior performance and sustainability.</p></p>
