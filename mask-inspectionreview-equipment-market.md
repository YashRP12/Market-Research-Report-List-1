<p><p>The Mask Inspection/Review Equipment Market consists of two main types: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the DD method, the equipment compares one die to another, highlighting any defects or errors. On the other hand, the DB method compares the die to a pre-established database, allowing for a more comprehensive analysis of the mask. Both methods enable efficient identification of flaws, providing valuable insights for manufacturers and ensuring high-quality production of electronic devices.</p></p>
