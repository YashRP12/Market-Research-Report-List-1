<p><ul><li>Dry Fermentation</li><li>Wet Fermentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-digesters are devices used for the decomposition of organic waste to produce biogas and biofertilizer. Dry fermentation bio-digesters are systems that process solid waste materials with low moisture content. They are suitable for agricultural residues, food waste, and similar substrates. Wet fermentation bio-digesters, on the other hand, process liquid waste or mixtures of liquid and solid waste. These systems are more suitable for sewage sludge, animal manure, or wastewater. Both types of bio-digesters offer sustainable waste management solutions, as they convert organic waste into useful energy and nutrient-rich byproducts.</p></p>
