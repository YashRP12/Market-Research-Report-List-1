<p><p>The Gamma Survey Meter Market comprises two main types: Geiger Counter Type and Ion Chamber Type. Geiger Counter Type is a widely used device that measures and detects gamma radiation by using a gas-filled detector and the principle of ionization. It is simple to operate, portable, and suitable for general radiation monitoring purposes. On the other hand, the Ion Chamber Type is designed for more precise and accurate measurements. It utilizes an ionization chamber that allows for detailed analysis of radiation levels, making it ideal for medical, research, and industrial applications.</p></p>
