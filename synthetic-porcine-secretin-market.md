<p><p>Synthetic porcine secretin is used in two main market types: exocrine pancreas function testing and Zollinger-Ellison syndrome testing. In exocrine pancreas function testing, synthetic porcine secretin is administered to measure the secretory capacity of the pancreas, helping diagnose conditions like chronic pancreatitis and pancreatic insufficiency. In Zollinger-Ellison syndrome testing, it is used to stimulate gastrin release and identify the presence of neuroendocrine tumors in the pancreas or duodenum. Both types play a crucial role in evaluating pancreatic function and diagnosing related disorders.</p></p>
