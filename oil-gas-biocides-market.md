<p><p>The Oil & Gas Biocides market consists of two main types: Oxidizing Fungicides and Non-Oxidizing Fungicides. Oxidizing Fungicides are chemicals that work by releasing oxygen radicals, which are highly reactive molecules that destroy or disable microorganisms. They are usually effective against a wide range of microbes but can sometimes cause corrosion issues. On the other hand, Non-Oxidizing Fungicides work by disrupting the cell walls or metabolic processes of microorganisms, preventing their growth and spread. They are generally less corrosive but may have limited effectiveness against certain types of microbes.</p></p>
