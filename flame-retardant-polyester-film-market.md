<p><p>The flame retardant polyester film market can be categorized into three types based on their thickness. Super-thin films are extremely thin, measuring below 6μm. Universal films fall between 6-65μm thickness range. Lastly, thick films are above 65μm in thickness. Each category caters to different applications and requirements. Super-thin films are ideal for compact and lightweight applications, while universal films offer versatility. Thick films provide durability and strength for heavy-duty applications. The varying thicknesses allow different industries to utilize flame retardant polyester films according to their specific needs.</p></p>
