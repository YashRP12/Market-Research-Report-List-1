<p><p>The animal probiotics market can be classified into three types based on the microorganisms used: Lactobacillus, Bacillus, and Yeast. Lactobacillus probiotics are derived from lactic acid bacteria and are commonly used in animal feed to improve gut health and digestion. Bacillus probiotics, originating from spore-forming bacteria, aid in enhancing immunity and controlling pathogens. Yeast probiotics are composed of various strains of Saccharomyces cerevisiae and are employed to enhance nutrient utilization and support animal growth. These different types of probiotics contribute to the overall diversity and effectiveness of animal probiotic products.</p></p>
