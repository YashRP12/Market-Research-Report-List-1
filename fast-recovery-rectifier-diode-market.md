<p><p>The Fast Recovery Rectifier Diode Market is categorized into two types: Single Configuration and Dual Configuration. Single Configuration diodes consist of a single PN junction and are capable of rectifying the current flow in only one direction. On the other hand, Dual Configuration diodes possess two PN junctions, enabling them to rectify current in both directions. These diodes are commonly used in electronic circuits that require rectification of alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) signals, such as power supplies and inverters.</p></p>
