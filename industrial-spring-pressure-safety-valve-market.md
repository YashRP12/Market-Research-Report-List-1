<p><p>The industrial spring pressure safety valve market can be categorized into three types: low pressure valves, medium pressure valves, and high pressure valves. Low pressure valves are designed to release pressure when it reaches a lower threshold, typically below 15 psi. Medium pressure valves are suitable for pressures ranging from 15 to 100 psi, while high pressure valves are capable of managing pressures exceeding 100 psi. These valves ensure the safety of industrial equipment by preventing excessive pressure buildup and potential damage to the system.</p></p>
