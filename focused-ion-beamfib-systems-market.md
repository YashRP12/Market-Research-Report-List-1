<p><p>Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Systems are advanced tools used in the semiconductor industry for materials analysis, circuit editing, and high-resolution imaging. These systems are extensively used in various applications such as failure analysis, sample preparation, and device modification.</p><p>The FIB Systems market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for miniaturized electronic devices and the need for more advanced imaging techniques.
