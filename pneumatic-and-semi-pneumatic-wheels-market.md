<p><p>Pneumatic and semi-pneumatic wheels are types of tires used in various applications. Full pneumatic (PR) wheels are made entirely of air-filled rubber, providing excellent shock absorption and cushioning. HD Solid Pneumatic (SPRT) wheels have a solid rubber construction with air pockets or holes, making them puncture-resistant while still offering some shock absorption. Super-Flex (SU) wheels have a combination of air-filled and foam-filled segments, providing a balance between cushioning and durability. These different wheel types cater to specific needs, offering various levels of shock absorption and puncture resistance.</p></p>
