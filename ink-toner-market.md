<p><p>The ink and toner market is divided into two major types: dye ink and pigment ink. Dye ink is a liquid-based ink that is quick-drying and provides vibrant colors, making it ideal for printing photos and images. Pigment ink, on the other hand, is made up of fine powder particles suspended in a liquid solution, providing a more fade-resistant and water-resistant result. Pigment ink is commonly used for printing text or documents that require long-lasting durability. Both types cater to different printing needs, ensuring high-quality and suitable results.</p></p>
