<p><p>Hand hygiene compliance monitoring solutions help promote proper hand hygiene practices in healthcare settings. There are three main types of monitoring solutions: activity monitoring, RTLS monitoring, and other market solutions.</p><p>Activity monitoring involves the use of sensors or wearable devices to track healthcare workers' hand hygiene activities. It can provide real-time alerts or reminders to improve compliance.</p><p>RTLS (Real-Time Location System) monitoring utilizes technologies like Bluetooth or RFID to monitor healthcare workers' hand hygiene practices. It can track movements and provide data on hand hygiene compliance.</p><p>Other market solutions refer to different approaches or technologies used to monitor hand hygiene compliance, such as hand hygiene education programs, video monitoring systems, or UV light-based systems that help identify missed areas during hand washing.</p></p>
