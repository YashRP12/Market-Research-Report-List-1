<p><ul><li>1+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+2 Power Configuration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The redundant power systems market comprises three main types of power configurations. The 1+1 power configuration involves one primary power source and one backup power source that can take over in case of a failure. The 2+1 power configuration consists of two primary power sources and one backup power source, offering an extra layer of redundancy. The 2+2 power configuration includes two sets of primary power sources and two backup power sources, providing the highest level of redundancy and ensuring uninterrupted power supply in case of any failure.</p></p>
