<p><p>The snowmobile tire market can be categorized into three types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and offer a comfortable ride due to their ability to absorb shocks. Solid tires, on the other hand, are made of rubber and do not require air, making them more durable and puncture-resistant. Polyurethane tires, made from a synthetic material, also offer durability and puncture-resistance, while providing a smoother ride. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs, offering options for varying terrains and conditions.</p></p>
