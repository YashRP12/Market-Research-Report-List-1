<p><strong>The Saloon Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Magnetic</li><li>Closed Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Saloon Ignition Coil market consists of two types: Open Magnetic and Closed Magnetic. Open Magnetic ignition coils have a visible iron core and are known for their simplicity and low cost. They are widely used in older or budget-friendly vehicles. On the other hand, Closed Magnetic ignition coils have a sealed iron core, offering better reliability and performance. They are commonly found in newer or high-end vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in generating the high-voltage electrical spark needed to ignite the fuel-air mixture in the engine's combustion chamber.</p></p>
