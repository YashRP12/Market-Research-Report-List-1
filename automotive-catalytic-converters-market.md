<p><ul><li>Two-Way Converters</li><li>Three-Way Converters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive catalytic converters are essential components in vehicles that help reduce harmful emissions. Two-way converters primarily target carbon monoxide (CO) and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) by converting them into carbon dioxide (CO2) and water. Three-way converters, on the other hand, also aim to reduce nitrogen oxides (NOx) in addition to CO and HC. These converters use a combination of catalysts to convert these pollutants into less harmful substances. The "Other" category comprises specialized catalytic converters used in specific applications, such as diesel oxidation catalysts or lean-burn converters.</p></p>
