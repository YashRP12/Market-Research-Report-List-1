The growing construction industry, particularly in emerging economies, is one of the key factors driving the demand for these fibers. The increasing focus on infrastructure development, urbanization, and sustainable construction practices is also expected to boost the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, synthetic concrete fibers offer several advantages over traditional reinforcement methods, such as steel bars or mesh. They are easier to handle and mix, provide better crack resistance and impact resistance, and have a higher resistance to chemicals and corrosion. These properties make synthetic concrete fibers a preferred choice for many construction applications.</p><p>Moreover, the introduction of advanced fiber technologies, such as macro fibers or hybrid fibers, is further improving the performance and application options of synthetic concrete fibers.
