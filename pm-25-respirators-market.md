<p><p>PM 2.5 respirators are designed to protect individuals from particulate matter of size 2.5 micrometers or smaller. There are two main types available in the market: valved and unvalved respirators. Valved respirators have an exhalation valve that enhances breathability by reducing moisture and heat buildup, offering more comfort during prolonged use. On the other hand, unvalved respirators lack the valve and provide higher filtration efficiency due to their sealed design. However, they may lead to increased resistance during breathing, potentially causing discomfort in some individuals.</p></p>
