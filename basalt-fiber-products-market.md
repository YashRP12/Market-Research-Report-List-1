<p><p>The Basalt Fiber Products Market includes two main types of products: Basalt Chopped Fiber and Basalt Continuous Fiber. Basalt Chopped Fiber is made by chopping basalt rocks into short lengths, which are then formed into different products such as insulation materials, reinforcement fabrics, and concrete additives. On the other hand, Basalt Continuous Fiber is produced by continuously pulling basalt rocks into thin fibers, which are then woven into fabrics or used as a reinforcement in composite materials. Both types of products have various applications in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
