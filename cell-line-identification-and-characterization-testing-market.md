<p><p>Cell line identification and characterization testing is used to authenticate and validate cell lines intended for research and development in the biopharmaceutical industry. Short Tandem Repeat (STR) analysis is a widely adopted technique that identifies unique DNA sequences in the cell line, ensuring its authenticity. Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) analysis detects variations in a single nucleotide, aiding in genetic profiling. Additionally, there are other methods employed, such as DNA sequencing and karyotyping, to verify cell line identity and detect any genetic aberrations that may impact research outcomes. These tests ensure the reliability and reproducibility of experimental results.</p></p>
