<p><p>The naval marine valve market consists of various types of valves used in marine applications. Straight through valves are designed to allow a straight flow of fluid, while once through valves enable the fluid to flow through the valve only once. Angle type valves have an angled body to change the direction of fluid flow. Plunger valves use a cylindrical or tapered plug to control the flow. These valve types play a crucial role in controlling fluid flow in different marine systems and applications.</p></p>
