<p><p>Satellite Digital Set-Top Box market is categorized into two types: OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT refers to the delivery of video and television content over the internet without the need for a traditional satellite or cable TV service. It allows users to access a wide range of content through streaming platforms like Netflix or Amazon Prime. On the other hand, DTT relies on terrestrial signals to transmit digital TV channels directly to users' set-top boxes, eliminating the need for satellite or cable infrastructure.</p></p>
