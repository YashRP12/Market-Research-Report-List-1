<p><ul><li>Antidegradants</li><li>Accelerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber processing chemicals, including antidegradants and accelerators, play a vital role in the manufacturing of rubber products. Antidegradants are substances that protect rubber from the harmful effects of heat, oxygen, and ozone, thereby extending the product's lifespan. Accelerators, on the other hand, speed up the curing process of rubber by increasing the rate of vulcanization, allowing for quicker production. Both types of chemicals are critical for enhancing the durability and performance of rubber goods, ensuring quality and longevity in various industries.</p></p>
