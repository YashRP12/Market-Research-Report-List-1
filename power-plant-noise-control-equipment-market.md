<p><p>The Power Plant Noise Control Equipment market encompasses various types of equipment used to reduce noise generated by power plants. Dust silencers are used to suppress the noise produced by dust collectors and filters. Acoustic louvers help to attenuate noise coming from power plant ventilators. Sound enclosures are structures designed to enclose noisy equipment and reduce their noise emissions. Sound barrier walls are erected around power plants to diminish noise transmission to surrounding areas. Mufflers are installed in exhaust systems of power plants to attenuate noise. Duct and pipe lagging and absorptive panels are used to reduce noise in ductwork and other areas of power plants.</p></p>
