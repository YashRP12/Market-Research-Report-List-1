<p><p>Hopper cars are a type of railroad freight car used for transporting bulk materials. There are two main market types: covered hopper cars and open hopper cars. Covered hopper cars have enclosed tops to protect the cargo from weather conditions, dust, and theft. They are commonly used for carrying grain, cement, and other sensitive products. On the other hand, open hopper cars have no roof and are suitable for transporting larger, durable materials such as coal, aggregates, and ores. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently transporting different types of bulk goods across the rail network.</p></p>
