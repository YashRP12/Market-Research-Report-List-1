<p><p>The automotive media entertainment market consists of two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket. OEM refers to the audio and video systems that are originally installed in a vehicle by the manufacturer. These systems are integrated with the vehicle's features and functions. On the other hand, aftermarket refers to the media entertainment systems that are purchased separately and installed in a vehicle after its purchase. Aftermarket systems offer more customization options and are often used to upgrade the existing OEM systems for a better audio-visual experience.</p></p>
