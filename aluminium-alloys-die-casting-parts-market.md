<p><p>In the Aluminium Alloys Die Casting Parts Market, two common types are Vacuum Die Casting and Pressure Die Casting. Vacuum Die Casting involves the use of a vacuum to remove air from the mold cavity before pouring the molten metal, resulting in a higher-quality finished product. On the other hand, Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure, allowing for more complex shapes and improved surface finish compared to other casting methods. Both techniques are widely used in the aluminium alloys die casting industry for various applications.</p></p>
