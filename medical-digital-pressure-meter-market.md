<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Medical Digital Pressure Meter market comprises three types: Positive Digital Pressure Meter, Vacuum Digital Pressure Meter, and Others. A Positive Digital Pressure Meter measures pressures that are higher than atmospheric pressure, providing accurate readings for monitoring blood pressure and vital signs. The Vacuum Digital Pressure Meter, on the other hand, is used to measure pressures lower than atmospheric pressure, finding application in respiratory therapy and vacuum suction systems. The category of Others includes digital pressure meters that serve specific medical purposes apart from positive or vacuum pressure measurement, such as monitoring pressurized fluids in medical devices.</p></p>
