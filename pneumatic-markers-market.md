<p><p>Pneumatic markers are divided into two main types: pneumatic benchtop markers and pneumatic portable markers. Pneumatic benchtop markers are designed for use on workbenches or stationary surfaces. They are typically larger and more powerful, allowing for precise and efficient marking. On the other hand, pneumatic portable markers are smaller and more lightweight, offering the flexibility to be easily transported and used in different locations. Both types of markers utilize pneumatic technology, which uses compressed air to create the marking force, resulting in accurate and permanent markings.</p></p>
