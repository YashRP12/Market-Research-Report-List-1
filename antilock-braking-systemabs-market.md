<p><p>Antilock Braking System (ABS) is a safety feature that prevents wheels from locking up during braking, thus ensuring better control and stability. The market types for ABS can be categorized as Four Leading, Three Leading, Two Leading, and One Leading. In a Four Leading market, all four wheels have individual ABS controls. In a Three Leading market, the rear wheels share one ABS control. In a Two Leading market, the front wheels share one ABS control. In a One Leading market, a single ABS control is used for all wheels. These market types reflect variations in ABS implementation across different vehicles.</p></p>
