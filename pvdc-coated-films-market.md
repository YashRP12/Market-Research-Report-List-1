<p><p>PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) coated films are a type of barrier packaging films that provide excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties. These films are primarily used in the food and pharmaceutical industries to package products that require extended shelf life, such as snacks, confectionery, meat, cheese, and drugs.</p><p>The PVDC coated films market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.00% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for packaged food products, especially in developing countries, is driving the demand for PVDC coated films. With the rising consumption of drugs and medicines, the need for effective packaging solutions that can protect the medicines from external factors such as moisture and oxygen is increasing.</p><p>Moreover, the shift towards sustainable and eco-friendly packaging materials is also driving the market growth.
