<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectrum analyzers are electronic devices used to measure and analyze signals in different frequency ranges. Swept-tuned spectrum analyzers scan a frequency range by tuning through it sequentially, providing accurate measurements but slower speed. Vector signal analyzers focus on analyzing complex-modulated signals, offering precise information about signal characteristics. Real-time spectrum analyzers capture and analyze signals continuously, providing immediate and accurate results. Each market type caters to different user requirements, with swept-tuned analyzers being cost-effective, vector signal analyzers targeting communication industries, and real-time analyzers suited for applications requiring instantaneous feedback.</p></p>
