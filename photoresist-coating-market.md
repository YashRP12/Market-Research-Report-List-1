<p><strong>The Photoresist Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Coating</li><li>Negative Photoresist Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist coating market is divided into two types: positive photoresist coating and negative photoresist coating. Positive photoresist coating is a type of material that becomes soluble when exposed to light, allowing the desired areas to be etched or developed. It is commonly used in the semiconductor industry for pattern transfer processes. Negative photoresist coating, on the other hand, becomes insoluble when exposed to light, protecting the desired areas, while the unexposed areas are etched or developed. It finds applications in the production of printed circuit boards and microfluidic devices.</p></p>
