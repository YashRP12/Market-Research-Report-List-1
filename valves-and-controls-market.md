<p><ul><li>Sliding-Stem Globe</li><li>V-notch Ball</li><li>Butterfly Types</li><li>Angle Types</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valves and controls market consists of various types such as sliding-stem globe valves, v-notch ball valves, butterfly valves, angle valves, and others. Sliding-stem globe valves regulate flow by linear motion of the stem, ensuring precise control. V-notch ball valves feature a V-shaped notch in the ball, providing accurate flow control in applications with fibrous or viscous fluids. Butterfly valves use a disc to regulate flow and are lightweight and cost-effective. Angle valves have an angled body, allowing for tight shutoff. Lastly, there are other valve types like gate valves and check valves, each designed for specific control requirements.</p></p>
