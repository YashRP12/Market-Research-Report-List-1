<p><strong>The Polycrystalline Diamond Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDSs</li><li>PDCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD) market includes two main types: Polycrystalline Diamond Segments (PDSs) and Polycrystalline Diamond Compacts (PDCs). PDSs are composed of small diamond grains sintered together, forming a strong and wear-resistant material widely used in cutting tools, mining, and oil industries. PDCs, on the other hand, consist of diamond grits bonded to a tungsten carbide substrate, offering high hardness and excellent wear resistance, making them suitable for drilling and machining applications in the oil and gas sector. Both PDSs and PDCs play crucial roles in various industries where durability and precision are highly valued.</p></p>
