There are three types of oxidation catalysts - precious metals, special promoters, and special binders. Precious metals, such as platinum, palladium, and rhodium, enhance the catalytic activity. Special promoters, like cerium and zirconium, further enhance the performance of precious metals. Special binders, such as alumina and silica, improve the stability and durability of the catalysts. These catalysts play a crucial role in various industries, including automotive, petroleum refining, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
