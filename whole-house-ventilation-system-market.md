<p><p>Whole-house ventilation systems come in different types such as ceiling and wall-mounted options. Ceiling ventilation systems are typically installed in the ceiling of a house and expel stale air from the living spaces. They usually feature filters to remove pollutants and maintain indoor air quality. On the other hand, wall-mounted ventilation systems are installed on walls and provide fresh air intake from the outdoors. They also incorporate filtration systems and help maintain a healthy and comfortable indoor environment by exchanging stale air with fresh air.</p></p>
