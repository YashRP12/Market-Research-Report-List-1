<p><p>The Elemental Analysis Appliance market can be divided into two main types: Stationary Analysis Appliance and Portable Analysis Appliance. On the other hand, Portable Analysis Appliances are smaller, lightweight instruments that can be easily carried to different locations for on-site elemental analysis.
