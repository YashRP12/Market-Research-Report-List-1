<p><p>The Automatic Opening Laparoscopic Retrieval Bag market is divided into two types: Detachable Retrieval Bags and Non-detachable Retrieval Bags. Detachable Retrieval Bags can be easily separated from the laparoscope after the procedure, enabling efficient collection and removal of the specimen. On the other hand, Non-detachable Retrieval Bags cannot be separated and are designed to directly extract the specimen from the body, reducing the risk of contamination and simplifying the retrieval process. Both types cater to the specific needs and preferences of surgeons during laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
