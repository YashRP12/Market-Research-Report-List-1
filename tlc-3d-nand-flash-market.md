The increasing demand for smartphones, tablets, and solid-state drives (SSDs) is a major driver for the market. The automotive industry is increasingly incorporating advanced features like infotainment systems, advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), and autonomous driving, which generate huge amounts of data that need to be stored and processed quickly. TLC 3D NAND Flash provides the necessary storage capacity and performance for these applications.</p><p>Another notable trend in the market is the shift towards QLC (Quad-Level Cell) NAND Flash.
