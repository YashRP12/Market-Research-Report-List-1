Coconut shell activated carbon is derived from coconut shells and is known for its high adsorption capacity, making it suitable for water and air purification applications. Shell activated carbon, produced from other plant shells, also exhibits excellent adsorption properties and finds applications in gas phase purification and wastewater treatment. Coal activated carbon, made from coal, is widely used in gas purification, solvent recovery, and gold extraction industries due to its high hardness and large surface area for adsorption.</p></p>
