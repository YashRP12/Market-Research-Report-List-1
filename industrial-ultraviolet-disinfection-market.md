<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The industrial ultraviolet disinfection market consists of three types: ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection, high-intensity ultraviolet disinfection, and ozone UV disinfection. Ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection utilizes low-pressure mercury lamps to produce ultraviolet light for disinfection purposes. High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection employs higher-output lamps to effectively disinfect larger volumes of water. Ozone UV disinfection combines UV light with ozone gas to provide a more powerful disinfection solution. These different types offer varying levels of effectiveness and application based on the specific disinfection requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
