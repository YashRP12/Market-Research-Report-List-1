<p><p>Plastic clip bearings are available in different market types, including double flange bearings and split bearings. Double flange bearings have flanges on both sides of the bearing, which provide increased stability and support. These bearings are commonly used in applications where there is axial and radial load. On the other hand, split bearings have a split design, allowing for easy installation and removal. They are used in situations where the bearing needs to be replaced frequently or when the shaft cannot be disassembled.</p></p>
