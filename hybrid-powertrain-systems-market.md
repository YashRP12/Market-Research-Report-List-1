<p><strong>The Hybrid Powertrain Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Series-parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid powertrain systems are gaining popularity in the automotive industry due to their improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. Parallel hybrid systems consist of an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, which can work together or independently to power the vehicle. Series hybrid systems use an electric motor to solely power the vehicle, with an internal combustion engine generating electricity. Series-parallel hybrid systems combine the features of both parallel and series systems, allowing the vehicle to operate in different modes, depending on the driving conditions.</p></p>
