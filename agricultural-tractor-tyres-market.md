<p><p>Agricultural tractor tires can be categorized into two main types: radial agriculture tires and bias (crossply) agriculture tires. Radial agriculture tires are constructed with a radial-ply design, which provides a larger footprint and better traction, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and reduced soil compaction. On the other hand, bias (crossply) agriculture tires have a diagonal-ply construction, offering enhanced load-carrying capacity and durability. While both types have their advantages, the choice depends on factors like soil conditions, equipment compatibility, and cost considerations.</p></p>
