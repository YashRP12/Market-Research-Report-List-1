<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Banknote-Printing Machine market can be categorized into three main types: Intaglio Printing, Offset Printing, and Others. Intaglio Printing is a traditional method that engraves the image onto a metal plate, which is then used to print the banknotes. Offset Printing utilizes a rubber cylinder to transfer the inked image onto a rubber blanket, which is then transferred to the banknote. The category of Others includes various modern printing techniques like gravure printing and letterpress printing, each with its own unique processes and characteristics for banknote production.</p></p>
