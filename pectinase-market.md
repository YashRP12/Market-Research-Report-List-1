<p><p>The pectinase market consists of different types of enzymes used in various industries. Protopectinases help break down protopectin, a tough substance in plant cell walls. Polygalacturonases break down pectin, a carbohydrate found in plant tissues. Pectin lyases are enzymes that cleave pectin molecules. Pectinesterase breaks down pectinester, a modified form of pectin. These enzymes are utilized in industries like food and beverage, textile, and pharmaceuticals, where they aid in processes such as juice extraction, fiber production, and drug formulation.</p></p>
