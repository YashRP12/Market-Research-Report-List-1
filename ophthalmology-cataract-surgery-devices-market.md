Phacoemulsification devices use ultrasound technology to break up the cloudy lens for easy removal. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices are used to maintain the shape of the eye during surgery and protect its structures. All these devices play a crucial role in modern cataract surgery by improving vision and ensuring successful outcomes for patients.</p></p>
