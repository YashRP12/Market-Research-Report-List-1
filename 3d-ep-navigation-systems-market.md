This system uses advanced imaging technologies to precisely track the catheter's position within the body. On the other hand, the contact mapping navigation system within the same market involves the direct physical contact between the catheter and the tissues to create a detailed map of the targeted area. These systems provide accurate guidance during various medical procedures.</p></p>
