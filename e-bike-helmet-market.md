<p><p>The E-Bike Helmet market consists of two main types: Full Face Helmet and Open Face Helmet. A Full Face Helmet provides complete coverage for the head, with a protective chin bar, making it suitable for off-road and high-speed riding. It offers enhanced protection against impacts and is commonly used by professional riders. On the other hand, an Open Face Helmet covers the head but leaves the face exposed. This type is popular among casual riders and urban commuters due to its convenience, better ventilation, and comfortable fit.</p></p>
