<p><p>SBS waterproofing membranes are widely used in construction projects to prevent water leakage and ensure the durability of structures. There are two common types of SBS membranes: fiberglass reinforced (G type) and polyester reinforced (PY type). The fiberglass reinforced membranes are enhanced with fiberglass strands, providing strength, stability, and resistance to tearing. On the other hand, polyester reinforced membranes are fortified with polyester fabric, offering superior flexibility and elongation properties. Both membranes are effective in waterproofing applications, with their specific characteristics catering to diverse project requirements.</p></p>
