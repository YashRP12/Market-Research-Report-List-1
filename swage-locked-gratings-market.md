<p><p>Swage locked gratings, also known as swage-locked bar gratings, are made by permanently connecting bearing bars with crossbars through the swaging process. This involves manipulating the individual bearing bars to interlock with crossbars, resulting in a rigid and durable grating structure. Swage locked gratings are commonly used in various industries, including energy, oil and gas, construction, and automotive, due to their stability, strength, and resistance to corrosion.</p><p>The market analysis of the swage locked gratings market indicates a positive growth trend.
