<p><p>Ductility testers are used to measure the ability of a material to deform under tensile stress. The market for ductility testers can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual testers require human intervention for operation, including sample preparation and loading. Semi-automatic testers automate some parts of the testing process, such as sample loading, while still requiring human intervention for other tasks. Automatic testers are fully automated, performing all testing processes without human interference. These different types cater to varying levels of automation and user involvement, providing options for different testing requirements.</p></p>
