<p><p>The metal polish market can be categorized into three main types: paste, liquid, and others. Paste metal polish is a thick, semi-solid substance that is applied to surfaces with a cloth or sponge. It is known for its ability to remove tough stains and provide a high level of shine. Liquid metal polish, on the other hand, is a thinner solution that can be applied with a cloth or sprayed directly onto surfaces. It is popular for its ease of use and quick drying time. The "others" category includes metal polish in different forms such as aerosols, sprays, and wipes.</p></p>
