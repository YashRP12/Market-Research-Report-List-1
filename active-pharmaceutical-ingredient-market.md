<p><p>The Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) market can be divided into two main types: Synthetic API and Biotech API. Synthetic API refers to the production of pharmaceutical ingredients through chemical synthesis in a laboratory. It involves the creation of a molecule from raw materials. On the other hand, Biotech API involves the use of living organisms (such as bacteria or yeast) or their components to produce pharmaceutical ingredients. This approach utilizes biological processes and techniques, such as recombinant DNA technology, to create the desired molecules.</p></p>
