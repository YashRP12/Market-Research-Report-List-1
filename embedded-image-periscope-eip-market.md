<p><p>Embedded Image Periscope (EIP) technology is used in various markets and can be classified into two types: penetrating type and non-penetrating type. </p><p>The penetrating type market involves applications where the EIP is directly inserted or integrated into the desired object or system, such as in medical imaging or military surveillance. This allows for accurate and detailed imaging from within the object or system.</p><p>On the other hand, the non-penetrating type market includes applications where the EIP is used externally, without physically entering the object or system. This can be seen in industrial inspections, agriculture, or underwater exploration, where the EIP's remote sensing capabilities provide valuable visual information without any physical interference.</p></p>
