<p><p>Hybrid Fiber-Coaxial (HFC) Cable Market refers to the telecommunications infrastructure that combines both fiber optic and coaxial cables. Single-mode fiber market involves transmitting data over a single strand of fiber, enabling long-distance, high-speed communication with minimal signal loss. Multimode fiber market, on the other hand, uses multiple strands of fiber to transmit data over shorter distances at lower speeds, making it suitable for local area networks. Both market types cater to different communication needs, with single-mode fiber being ideal for long-distance applications and multimode fiber being more suitable for shorter-distance networks.</p></p>
