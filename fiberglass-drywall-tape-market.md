<p><p>Fiberglass drywall tape is available in different types, including silicone coated and vermiculite coated options. Silicone coated fiberglass drywall tapes have a layer of silicone applied to the surface, which provides strength and durability. These tapes are resistant to mold and moisture, making them suitable for damp areas. On the other hand, vermiculite coated fiberglass drywall tapes are coated with a layer of vermiculite, a material that expands when exposed to heat to provide fire resistance. This type of tape is commonly used in fire-rated walls or ceilings for added safety.</p></p>
