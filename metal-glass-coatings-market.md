<p><p>The Metal & Glass Coatings Market can be divided into three types: solventborne, waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings are solvent-based and are widely used due to their excellent adhesion and durability. Waterborne coatings, on the other hand, are water-based and have gained popularity due to their eco-friendly properties and low VOC emissions. UV coatings are cured by ultraviolet light and offer high gloss, scratch resistance, and fast curing. Each type serves specific purposes and caters to different customer requirements in the metal and glass coatings market.</p></p>
