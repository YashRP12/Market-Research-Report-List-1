<p><p>Zirconium oxide, also known as zirconia, is a white crystalline oxide of zirconium metal. In the healthcare industry, zirconium oxide is used in dental crowns and implants due to its biocompatibility and aesthetic appeal.</p><p>According to market analysis, the zirconium oxide market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
