<p><p>Process gas centrifugal compressors are used in various industries for compressing gases in different stages. The Single Stage market refers to compressors that have a single impeller to increase gas pressure. They are typically used for low-pressure ratio applications. On the other hand, Multi-Stage compressors have multiple impellers arranged in series to achieve higher pressure ratios. These compressors are suitable for high-pressure ratio applications and provide better efficiency, reliability, and flexibility for handling different gases. Both Single Stage and Multi-Stage compressors play key roles in meeting the diverse compression requirements of various industries.</p></p>
