<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Ceramic Bone Cement is classified into three types based on viscosity: Low Viscosity Cements, Medium Viscosity Cements, and High Viscosity Cements. Low Viscosity Cements are fluid and easily flow into the bone cavities, providing excellent penetration. Medium Viscosity Cements have a moderate flowability that allows for better handling during surgery while ensuring sufficient penetration. High Viscosity Cements have a thick consistency, offering better control during application and reduced risk of cement leakage. Each type of cement serves specific purposes in bone surgeries, catering to different patient needs and surgical requirements.</p></p>
