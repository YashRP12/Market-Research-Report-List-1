<p><p>Floodlight projectors are available in two main types: Metal-halide and LED. Metal-halide projectors use a metal-halide lamp to produce light. These lamps contain a mixture of metal halides and emit a bright, white light. Metal-halide projectors are known for their high-intensity illumination and wide coverage area. On the other hand, LED projectors use light-emitting diodes to generate light. LED projectors have gained popularity due to their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and versatility in color options. They offer a reliable and cost-effective solution for various lighting applications.</p></p>
