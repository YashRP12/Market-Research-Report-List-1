<p><p>Welded blister packing refers to a type of packaging where blisters are sealed using various welding techniques. High frequency welded blisters use high-frequency electromagnetic waves for sealing. Radio frequency (RF) welded blisters use radio waves for sealing. Ultrasonic welded blisters use high-frequency ultrasonic vibrations for sealing. Thermowelded blisters use heat and pressure for sealing. These different welding techniques allow for secure and tamper-proof packaging solutions for various industries, ensuring the safety and longevity of the products being packaged.</p></p>
