Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive coating on only one side and are commonly used for bonding or fastening objects. In contrast, double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive coating on both sides and are typically used for mounting or joining two surfaces together. Both types of UV resistant tape offer protection against UV rays in various applications.</p></p>
