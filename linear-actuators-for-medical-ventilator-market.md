<p><p>Linear actuators for the medical ventilator market are classified into two types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic linear actuators utilize compressed air to generate linear motion, offering advantages such as high force capabilities and fast response times. On the other hand, electric linear actuators use electric motors to convert electrical energy into linear movement, providing precise control and adjustable speed settings. Both types cater to the medical ventilator market's need for reliable and efficient mechanical systems to ensure proper ventilation for patients.</p></p>
