<p><p>Arrhythmia management devices are medical devices used in the diagnosis and treatment of abnormal heart rhythms, also known as arrhythmias. These devices include implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), pacemakers, and cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) devices.</p><p>ICDs are used to monitor heart rhythms and deliver an electric shock to restore normal rhythm in case of life-threatening arrhythmias. Pacemakers, on the other hand, regulate heartbeats by delivering electrical impulses at a constant rate. CRT devices are primarily used to treat heart failure patients with arrhythmias by synchronizing the contractions of the heart's lower chambers.</p><p>The future outlook of the arrhythmia management devices market is expected to be promising.
