<p><p>Carbon Nanotubes (CNT) are cylindrical carbon structures with exceptional properties, making them valuable in various industries. There are two types of CNT markets: Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNTs) and Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNTs). MWCNTs consist of concentric nanotubes and have high tensile strength, making them useful in aerospace and electronics sectors. SWCNTs, on the other hand, have a single wall and possess unique electrical and thermal properties, leading to their application in nanoelectronics, energy storage, and biomedical fields. These markets offer diverse opportunities for research, development, and commercialization of CNT-based products.</p></p>
