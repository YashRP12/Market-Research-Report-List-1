<p><p>Black granite is a type of igneous rock predominantly composed of silica, along with various other minerals such as feldspar, amphiboles, and micas. Black granite is widely used in applications like countertops, flooring, cladding, tombstones, and monuments due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.
