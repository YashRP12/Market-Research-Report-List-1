<p><p>A Vehicle Cabin Air Quality Sensor is a device installed in vehicles to monitor and maintain the air quality within the cabin. The sensor provides real-time data to the vehicle's air filtration systems, allowing them to adjust and purify the air as necessary, ensuring a healthier and more comfortable environment for the occupants.</p><p>The global Vehicle Cabin Air Quality Sensor market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing awareness about the harmful effects of air pollution and the rising demand for vehicle safety and comfort are major factors driving this growth. For instance, there is a rising demand for multi-functional cabin air quality sensors that not only detect pollution but also monitor humidity, temperature, and pollution sources.
