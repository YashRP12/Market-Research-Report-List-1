<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Low-power bridges in the market can be categorized into three types: I2C to SPI, SPI to I2C, and other bridges. An I2C to SPI bridge allows communication between devices using the Inter-Integrated Circuit (I2C) protocol and devices using the Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI) protocol. Conversely, an SPI to I2C bridge enables communication between devices using SPI and devices using I2C. The "other" category includes bridges that facilitate communication between different protocols, such as UART to I2C or UART to SPI, offering a flexible solution for interfacing diverse devices with low power consumption.</p></p>
