<p><p>A bidirectional ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) suppressor is a device designed to provide protection against electrostatic discharge, which is a momentary electrical shock caused by the transfer of static electricity between two objects. This type of suppressor is capable of protecting electronic components from both positive and negative voltage transients.</p><p>The bidirectional ESD suppressor market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of electronic devices and components in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications is driving the demand for ESD protection solutions. The rising awareness about the detrimental effects of electrostatic discharge on electronic components is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the advancements in technology and the continuous miniaturization of electronic devices are creating a need for more effective ESD protection solutions. Bidirectional ESD suppressors offer improved protection compared to unidirectional suppressors, making them ideal for safeguarding sensitive electronic components.</p><p>Additionally, the growing focus on the development of smart devices, wearables, and Internet of Things (IoT) devices is expected to further propel the market growth.
