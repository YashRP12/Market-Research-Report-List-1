<p><p>Paper composite panels are a type of building material that is made by compressing paper fibers together with a resin. Waterproof paper composite panels are designed specifically to resist moisture and are commonly used in areas that are prone to water exposure, such as bathrooms or kitchens. On the other hand, conventional paper composite panels do not have a waterproof coating and are typically used in dry environments where moisture is not a concern, such as offices or living rooms.</p></p>
