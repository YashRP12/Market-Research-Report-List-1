<p><p>The Precious Metal Flotation Reagents market is composed of three types: Collectors, Activators, and Inhibitors. Collectors enhance the attachment of precious metal particles to air bubbles, increasing their upward movement during flotation. Activators improve the adsorption of collectors on the mineral surface, optimizing the flotation process. Inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent unwanted particles from attaching to the air bubbles, minimizing their interference during flotation. These reagents play a vital role in separating precious metals from ores, contributing to the efficiency and effectiveness of flotation processes.</p></p>
