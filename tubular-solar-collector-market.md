<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The tubular solar collector market consists of two main types: all glass vacuum tubes and glass-metal vacuum tubes. All glass vacuum tubes are made entirely of glass and have a double-wall structure with a vacuum in between, which enhances their insulation properties. Glass-metal vacuum tubes, on the other hand, have a metal absorber coated with selective coatings, which improves their absorption efficiency. Both types are widely used in solar thermal applications, harnessing the sun's energy for heating water or air.</p></p>
