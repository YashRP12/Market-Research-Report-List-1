The market is expected to witness steady growth due to increasing demand for effective and innovative packaging solutions from various industries such as food and beverages, healthcare, cosmetics, electronics, and retail. These displays can be easily transported, assembled, and dismantled, making them suitable for online retailers who often need to ship products directly to customers, as well as brick-and-mortar stores.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing focus on sustainability and eco-friendly packaging solutions is expected to propel market growth.
