<p><p>The prepainted steel coil market can be categorized into four types: PE prepainted, HDP prepainted, SMP prepainted, and PVDF prepainted. PE prepainted refers to pre-painted steel coils coated with polyester paint, which provides good weather resistance and color durability. HDP prepainted stands for high-durability polyester, offering superior corrosion resistance and outdoor durability. SMP prepainted refers to silicon-modified polyester coatings that provide excellent weather resistance and are suitable for harsh environments. PVDF prepainted represents coils coated with polyvinylidene fluoride, known for its exceptional color retention and resistance against fading, chalking, and cracking.</p></p>
