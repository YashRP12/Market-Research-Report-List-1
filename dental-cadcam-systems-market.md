Firstly, the increasing prevalence of dental disorders and the growing demand for cosmetic dentistry are driving the adoption of CAD/CAM systems. Moreover, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to dental problems, is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements in dental CAD/CAM systems are revolutionizing the field of dentistry. The introduction of chairside CAD/CAM systems has allowed dentists to provide same-day restorations, eliminating the need for multiple visits and temporaries.
