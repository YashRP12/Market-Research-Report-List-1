<p><strong>The Metal Casting in Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC)</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC)</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the automotive industry, metal casting is a widely used manufacturing process for producing various components. Gravity casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold, allowing it to solidify under gravity. High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC) uses a high-pressure injection to fill the mold with molten metal. Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC) involves using low pressure to fill the mold. Sand casting, on the other hand, uses a mold made of sand to create the desired shape by pouring molten metal into it. These casting methods are popular due to their efficiency and ability to produce complex automotive parts.</p></p>
