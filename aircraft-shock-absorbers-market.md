<p><p>The Aircraft Shock Absorbers Market is divided into two main types: Mechanical Type and Hydraulic Type. Mechanical shock absorbers utilize a mechanical spring system to absorb and dampen the impact and vibrations generated during landing or takeoff. On the other hand, hydraulic shock absorbers rely on a fluid-filled chamber to provide cushioning and dampening capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining aircraft stability, enhancing passenger comfort, and protecting the aircraft structure from excessive stress and damage.</p></p>
