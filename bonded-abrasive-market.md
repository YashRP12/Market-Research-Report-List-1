<p><p>The bonded abrasive market is categorized into two types: natural and synthetic. Natural bonded abrasives are derived from minerals like garnet, emery, and quartz. They are known for their durability and are commonly used in applications such as grinding and polishing. On the other hand, synthetic bonded abrasives are manufactured using materials like ceramic, silicon carbide, and aluminum oxide. These abrasives offer higher strength and consistency, making them suitable for precision cutting and shaping in industries like automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
