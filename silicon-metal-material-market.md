<p><p>The silicon metal material market can be categorized into two main types: metallurgy grade and chemical grade. Metallurgy grade silicon metal is primarily used in the production of aluminum-silicon alloys for the automotive and construction industries. It possesses high thermal conductivity and good mechanical properties. On the other hand, chemical grade silicon metal is mainly utilized in the production of silicone compounds for applications in various industries such as electronics, cosmetics, and healthcare. It offers high purity levels and is crucial for the manufacturing of semiconductors and solar panels.</p></p>
