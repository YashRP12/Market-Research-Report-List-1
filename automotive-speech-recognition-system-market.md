<p><p>The Automotive Speech Recognition System market consists of two major types: Embedded and Hybrid market. Embedded speech recognition systems are built into the vehicle and have their own dedicated hardware, making them more reliable and accurate. On the other hand, Hybrid speech recognition systems combine both embedded and cloud-based technologies, allowing for a more flexible and intelligent user experience. While embedded systems provide quick responses and better privacy, hybrid systems offer enhanced understanding and access to a wider range of services through cloud connectivity.</p></p>
