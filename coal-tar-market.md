<p><p>The coal tar market can be categorized into three types: high temperature coal tar, medium temperature coal tar, and low temperature coal tar. High temperature coal tar is obtained by heating coal at high temperatures, resulting in a dark-colored liquid used in the production of electrodes, carbon black, and other industrial applications. Medium temperature coal tar is obtained at lower temperatures and finds usage in the construction industry for producing asphalt and road surfaces. Low temperature coal tar is produced at even lower temperatures and is used in wood preservation treatments and as a fuel in heating systems.</p></p>
