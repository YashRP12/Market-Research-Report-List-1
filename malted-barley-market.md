<p><p>The malted barley market can be categorized into two main types: basic malt and special malt. Basic malt is the most common type and is widely used in various food and beverage industries. It undergoes a standard malting process and provides a neutral flavor and color to the end product. On the other hand, special malt refers to malt that has been subjected to specific processing techniques or has unique characteristics, such as caramel or roasted malt.
