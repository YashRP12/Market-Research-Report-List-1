<p><p>The automotive driving recorder market includes two types: front dash cameras and rear dash cameras. Front dash cameras are installed on the front windshield of a vehicle and capture footage of the road ahead. These cameras help protect drivers from accidents, provide evidence in case of disputes, and enable monitoring of driving behavior. On the other hand, rear dash cameras are mounted on the back windshield or license plate of a vehicle and record footage of what is happening behind the car. These cameras aid in parking, reversing, and enhancing overall road safety.</p></p>
