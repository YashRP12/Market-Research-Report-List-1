<p><p>The ice hockey stick market can be categorized into two types: glass fiber and carbon fiber. Glass fiber sticks are made from woven glass fiber sheets that are layered and compressed with a resin. These sticks offer good durability and affordability. On the other hand, carbon fiber sticks are constructed using woven carbon fiber sheets layered and compressed with a resin. They are known for their lightweight nature, high strength, and enhanced performance. Carbon fiber sticks are generally more expensive compared to glass fiber sticks.</p></p>
