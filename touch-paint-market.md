<p><p>There are two main types of Touch Paint available in the market: water-based Touch Paint and solvent-based Touch Paint. Water-based Touch Paint is made by mixing water with pigments and binders, resulting in a non-toxic and easy-to-clean product. It is widely used in residential and educational settings due to its safety and convenience. Solvent-based Touch Paint, on the other hand, uses solvents as a base, making it more durable and suitable for industrial and commercial applications. However, it may contain harmful chemicals and requires proper ventilation during application.</p></p>
