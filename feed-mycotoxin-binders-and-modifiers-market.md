<p><p>Feed mycotoxin binders and modifiers are substances added to animal feed to mitigate the negative effects of mycotoxins, which are toxic compounds produced by fungi that contaminate the feed. Binders are absorbents that bind to mycotoxins, preventing their absorption in animals' digestive systems. Modifiers, on the other hand, work by transforming mycotoxins into less harmful forms. Both binders and modifiers help improve the safety and nutritional quality of animal feed, ensuring the health and productivity of livestock.</p></p>
