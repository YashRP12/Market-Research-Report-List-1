<p><p>The radiation shielding wall market offers various types of products to protect against radiation, including lead lined sheetrock, lead plywood, and lead bricks. Lead lined sheetrock consists of gypsum board with a layer of lead to block radiation. Lead plywood is made by sandwiching lead between layers of plywood, providing sturdy radiation protection. Lead bricks are solid blocks made of lead, which are stacked together to form walls. These shielding materials are widely used in medical facilities, industrial plants, and nuclear power plants to minimize the risk of radiation exposure.</p></p>
