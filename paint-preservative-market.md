<p><ul><li>In Can Preservatives</li><li>Dry Film Presevatives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint preservative market can be divided into two types: in can preservatives and dry film preservatives. In can preservatives are used to protect the paint when it is in the can or container, preventing any growth of bacteria, fungi, or other microorganisms that may spoil the paint. On the other hand, dry film preservatives are applied directly to the painted surface, providing long-term protection against the growth of microorganisms and extending the lifespan of the paint. Both types of preservatives play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and durability of paint products.</p></p>
