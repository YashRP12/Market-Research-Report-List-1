<p><p>Control Flow Chokes are used in oil and gas industries for regulating and controlling fluid flow during well testing and production. Adjustable Chokes allow for variable adjustment of the orifice size, providing flexibility in managing flow rates and pressures. Fixed Chokes, on the other hand, have a fixed orifice size and are used when a specific flow rate or pressure needs to be maintained consistently. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient operations in the oil and gas sector by controlling the flow of fluids through the system.</p></p>
