<p><p>Medical ultrasound imaging equipment market includes different types of ultrasound technologies, such as 2D, 3D, and 4D imaging, as well as Doppler ultrasound. </p><p>- 2D ultrasound provides a two-dimensional image of the internal organs or tissues, allowing physicians to visualize the structure, size, and movement.</p><p>- 3D ultrasound produces a three-dimensional image by capturing multiple 2D images from different angles, enabling a more detailed visualization of organs and structures.</p><p>- 4D ultrasound adds the element of time to the 3D imaging, providing a live video-like view of the fetus or organ movement.</p><p>- Doppler ultrasound utilizes the Doppler effect to measure and analyze the speed and direction of blood flow, aiding in the diagnosis of vascular conditions or monitoring fetal health.</p></p>
