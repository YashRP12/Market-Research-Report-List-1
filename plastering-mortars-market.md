<p><strong>The Plastering Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Premixed Mounting Mortars</li><li>Premixed Rush Coats</li><li>Premixed Plasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastering mortars market includes various types such as premixed mounting mortars, premixed rush coats, and premixed plasters. Premixed mounting mortars are used for fixing heavy stones or tiles on walls. Premixed rush coats are applied as the first coat to even out surfaces before plastering. Premixed plasters, on the other hand, are used for the final finishing layer to achieve a smooth and appealing surface. These mortars play an essential role in construction projects by providing strong adhesion, leveling, and aesthetic benefits to walls and surfaces.</p></p>
