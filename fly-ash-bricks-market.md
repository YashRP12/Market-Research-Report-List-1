<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Fly ash bricks are classified into two types - Class F and Class C fly ash bricks, based on their composition and characteristics. Class F fly ash bricks are produced from fly ash generated by burning anthracite or bituminous coal and have low calcium content. These bricks are commonly used for non-load bearing walls and in landscaping applications. On the other hand, Class C fly ash bricks are made from fly ash with high calcium content obtained from burning sub-bituminous coal. They possess higher strength and are suitable for load-bearing structures such as walls and columns in construction projects.</p></p>
