<p><p>The X-ray Particle Analyzer market includes two main types: X-ray Diffraction (XRD) and X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) Type. XRD is a technique that examines the crystal structure and composition of particles by measuring the angle and intensity of X-ray scattered from the sample. It can identify crystalline phases present in a sample. On the other hand, XRF analyzes the elemental composition of particles using X-ray emission. It can provide information about the concentration and distribution of elements in a sample. Both techniques are widely used in scientific research, material analysis, and quality control applications.</p></p>
