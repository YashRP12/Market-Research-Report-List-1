<p><strong>The Polyvinylidenechloride Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDC Resins</li><li>PVDC Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvinylidenechloride (PVDC) market consists of two main types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid materials used for coating various surfaces, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture, gases, and odors. They are commonly employed in food packaging, electrical insulation, and corrosion-resistant coatings. On the other hand, PVDC latex refers to a water-based emulsion or dispersion of PVDC resins. This form offers similar barrier characteristics but is primarily used for coating paper or fabric. Both PVDC resins and PVDC latex play vital roles in enhancing the performance and durability of different products in various industries.</p></p>
