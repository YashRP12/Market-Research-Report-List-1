<p><ul><li>Slewing Ring Bearings</li><li>Spherical Roller Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The wind power bearing market is categorized into two types: slewing ring bearings and spherical roller bearings. Slewing ring bearings are designed to handle both axial and radial loads, allowing them to support the rotors in wind turbines. On the other hand, spherical roller bearings are specifically meant to handle high radial loads, making them suitable for use in the gearbox and main shaft of wind turbines. Both types of bearings play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of wind power systems.</p></p>
