<p><p>The Electric Spindle market can be classified into various types based on the motor mechanism. Rolling Motor Spindles employ rolling bearings for stable rotations and are commonly used in applications that require high precision. Air Bearing Motor Spindles utilize a thin film of air to support the rotating shaft, enabling ultra-precise and frictionless motion. Liquid Journal Motor Spindles use a liquid lubricant to reduce friction within the bearings. Additionally, there are other types of Electric Spindles available in the market, each designed to meet specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
