<p><p>The automotive automatic clutch market comprises two main types: Mechanical Motor Automatic Clutch and Hydraulic Automatic Clutch. Mechanical Motor Automatic Clutch refers to a clutch system that uses mechanical devices, such as gears and levers, to engage and disengage the clutch. On the other hand, Hydraulic Automatic Clutch utilizes hydraulic pressure to control the clutch operation. Both types serve the purpose of smoothly transmitting power from the engine to the transmission system, but they differ in terms of their mechanisms and technologies used.</p></p>
