<p><p>The explosion-proof LED drivers market consists of three types: 0-10V, CLK, and PWM. The 0-10V LED drivers use a voltage signal between 0 and 10 volts to control the brightness of LEDs. CLK LED drivers are based on Constant Light Output (CLK) technology, maintaining consistent light intensity regardless of input voltage fluctuations. PWM LED drivers utilize Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) technique to adjust LED brightness by controlling the width of the electrical pulse supplied to the LED. These types of LED drivers are specifically designed to prevent explosions in hazardous environments, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
