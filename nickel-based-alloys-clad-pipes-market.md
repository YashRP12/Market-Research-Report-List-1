<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Nickel-based alloys clad pipes are used in various industries due to their superior corrosion resistance properties. The market for these pipes can be segregated into three types based on the cladding process used. The roll bonding process involves hot rolling of the cladding material onto the base pipe. Mechanically bonded pipes involve mechanically bonding the cladding material to the base pipe. The explosive bonding process uses high energy explosives to bond the cladding material to the base pipe. Weld overlay process involves depositing the cladding material onto the base pipe through welding.</p></p>
