<p><p>Acrylic sheet market can be categorized into two main types: cast acrylic sheet and extruded acrylic sheet. The cast acrylic sheet is produced by pouring a liquid form of acrylic into a mold, resulting in a higher quality and clarity with a higher cost. On the other hand, the extruded acrylic sheet is formed by pushing acrylic pellets through a heated die, ensuring a more cost-effective solution but with slightly less clarity. Both types serve various applications and industries, including signage, furniture, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
