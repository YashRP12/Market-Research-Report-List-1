<p><p>Kinetic Energy Recovery System (KERS) is an advanced technology used in vehicles to recover and utilize the energy produced during braking. ESC Based System, one type of KERS, uses the electric stability control system to store energy from braking in a flywheel or battery, which can be used later to power the vehicle. ABS Based System, another type, utilizes the anti-lock braking system to capture and store energy. Other systems, such as hydraulic or mechanical KERS, exist as well, although they are less common in the market.</p></p>
