<p><p>A Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD) is a wearable device that presents information to the user in their field of view. They provide real-time information, augmented reality (AR), and virtual reality (VR) experiences, enhancing situational awareness, training, and overall performance.</p><p>The Helmet-Mounted Display Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for these devices in defense and aviation sectors. <p><p>The Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD) market comprises three types: Slide-on HMD, Discrete HMD, and Integrated HMD. Slide-on HMD refers to a device that attaches to an existing helmet, providing a display system. Discrete HMD is a stand-alone unit mounted directly on a helmet, offering enhanced viewing capabilities. Integrated HMD is a display system designed within the helmet itself. All three types offer various features and benefits, catering to different user requirements and scenarios. The market for these HMD types is driven by advancements in technology, increasing demand for military and aerospace applications, and growing interest in augmented reality devices.</p></p>
