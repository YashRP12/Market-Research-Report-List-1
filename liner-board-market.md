Kraft liner is made from wood pulp and has higher strength and durability. Kraft faced paper, also made from wood pulp, has a kraft liner on one side and a printable surface on the other. Ordinary liner, on the other hand, is made from recycled paper and is less strong and durable compared to the Kraft types. These types serve different purposes based on their strength and printing capabilities.</p></p>
