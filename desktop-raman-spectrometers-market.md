<p><p>Desktop Raman spectrometers come in two different types based on their power source: battery power type and external power supply type. </p><p>Battery power type desktop Raman spectrometers are equipped with built-in rechargeable batteries, allowing them to operate independently without the need for an external power source. This makes them portable and suitable for fieldwork or locations without easy access to power outlets.</p><p>On the other hand, external power supply type desktop Raman spectrometers require a direct connection to a power outlet. They do not include built-in batteries, making them less mobile but generally more powerful and suitable for laboratory settings where a consistent power source is readily available.</p></p>
