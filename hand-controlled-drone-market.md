<p><p>The hand-controlled drone market consists of two main types: remote control and gesture control. On the other hand, in the gesture control market, drones are operated by detecting and interpreting hand movements and gestures of the user. Both remote control and gesture control offer unique and exciting ways to operate drones.</p></p>
