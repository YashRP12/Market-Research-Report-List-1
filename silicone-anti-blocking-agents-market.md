<p><p>Silicone anti-blocking agents are substances that are added to materials, such as polymers and coatings, to reduce the adhesion between surfaces. The rising consumer awareness regarding the necessity of convenient and user-friendly products is also driving the demand for silicone anti-blocking agents.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing technological advancements leading to the development of innovative and high-performance anti-blocking agents.
